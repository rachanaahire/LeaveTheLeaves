About Us


This group was founded in 1970 by group of few tribal people and then later lead by a Journalist Dr. Kootrapali. Dr Kootrapali later came up with the name "Leave the Leaves" and made the group official and legal.

The initiative of the group is to save the trees from being cut and making people aware of the consequences of such causes. We aim to spread awareness about environment and nature with the motive of planting one plant a day.

Our Volunteers are trained to help others to spread knowledge about the cause.
We travel place to place with our volunteers to make people aware of how trees are important for us, making sure that no place is left. In the end we are the people who think about future and what we can do to make it less complicated.
